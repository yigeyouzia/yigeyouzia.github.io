==2.3 梯度下降==

理解

公式

$$
w=w-a \frac{\partial J(w)}{\partial w}
$$

  • 对多个w重复计算

  • 简化版本

  • $$
    \begin{array}{l}
    \text { repeat until convergence { }\
    \begin{aligned}
    w & =w-\alpha \frac{1}{m} \sum_{i=1}^{m}\left(f_{w, b}\left(x^{(i)}\right)-y^{(i)}\right) x^{(i)} \
    b & =b-\alpha \frac{1}{m} \sum_{i=1}^{m}\left(f_{w, b}\left(x^{(i)}\right)-y^{(i)}\right)
    \end{aligned}
    \end{array}
    $$
